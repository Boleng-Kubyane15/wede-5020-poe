## Project Overview

Tshwane Artisan Bakery is a family‑run business that opened its doors in Pretoria back in 2010. What started as a small kitchen operation has grown into a well‑loved local bakery known for its handcrafted bread, pastries, and custom cakes. Their philosophy is simple: baking is both an art and a tradition. The bakery’s mission is to bring warmth and flavour to every table, while their vision is to become Pretoria’s go‑to destination for authentic artisan baked goods. Their customers range from everyday locals who pop in for fresh bread to event planners looking for custom cakes.

## Website Goals and Objectives

The goals are to:
Showcase their products in a way that makes people hungry just by looking.

Make it easy for customers to place inquiries or orders.

Strengthen their brand identity and highlight their story.

Provide clear contact details and directions to the shop.

We will know if the website is working well if more people visit it,more customers sends us messages or oders online and more people sign up for our mailing list.

## Timeline and Milestones


Weeks 1–2: Research and gather content (photos of products, bakery story).

Weeks 3–4: Build the basic HTML structure and navigation.

Weeks 5–6: Add CSS styling and make the site responsive.

Weeks 7–8: Add JavaScript features (forms, animations).

Week 9: Test the site on different devices and browsers.

Week 10: Deploy the site and submit final version.
