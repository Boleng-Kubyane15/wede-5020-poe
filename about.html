About Us

Founded in 2010, Tshwane Artisan Bakery began as a small family kitchen in Pretoria. Over the years, we have grown into a beloved local bakery, known for our passion for handcrafted bread, pastries, and custom cakes.

Our mission is to bring warmth and flavour to every table. We believe baking is both an art and a tradition, and we are committed to using only the finest ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible.

Unlike mass-produced baked goods, every loaf and pastry is made with care, using time-honoured techniques that give our products their unique taste and texture.

Our team of passionate bakers and decorators treat every product as a labour of love. From sourdough bread to wedding cakes, we put our heart into everything we make.
