Our Products

At Tshwane Artisan Bakery, we don’t just bake — we create experiences that bring people together, we take pride in offering a wide range of freshly baked goods. Each product is made with care, using traditional methods and the finest ingredients.

Breads

  • Sourdough Loaf: Crusty and tangy, perfect for any meal.
  • Whole Wheat Bread: Wholesome and nutritious, baked daily.
  • French Baguette: Light, airy, and golden brown.

Pastries

  • Croissants: Buttery and flaky, baked fresh each morning.
  • Cinnamon Rolls: Sweet and spiced, topped with icing.
  • Fruit Danishes: Filled with seasonal fruits.
